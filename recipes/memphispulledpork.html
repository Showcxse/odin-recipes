Memphis Pulled Pork

Description

Pulled pork served in sandwhich form is a savory southern classic, perfect for a 4th of July, tailgate, cookout, or any other event. It takes some time for the pork to cook, but the end result is well worth it.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 2 (14 ounce) cans beef broth
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup cidar vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (4 pound) Boston butt pork shoulder roast
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons water

Steps

  1. Spread onion slices and garlic cloves in the bottom of a slow cooker; stir in beef broth, barbeque sauce, brown sugar, cider vinegar, and pepper. Add pork roast.
  2. Cover and cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Remove pork from slow cooker. Strain juices and return to the slow cooker. Mix water and cornstarch in a bowl; stir into juices until sauce is thickened.
